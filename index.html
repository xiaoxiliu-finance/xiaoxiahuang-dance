About

以身体书写诗篇Writing Poetry with the Body

黄晓夏，舞蹈编导，舞蹈教师。现任清华大学艺术教育中心舞蹈教师，清华大学学生无界舞蹈剧场（THU-BDT）指导教师。

她相信身体是最诚实的语言，每一次呼吸都是一首诗。在舞台与生活的交界处，她用肢体编织光影，将人生的悲欢离合化为空气中流动的诗篇。

她的作品融合东方美学与当代舞蹈语汇，带领一群来自清华各院系、因热爱舞蹈而相聚的学生，在传统与现代之间寻找属于自己的艺术表达。

Xiaoxia Huang is a choreographer and dance educator. She currently serves as a dance instructor at Tsinghua University's Art Education Center and faculty advisor of Tsinghua Boundless Dance Theatre (THU-BDT).

She believes the body is the most honest language, and every breath is a poem. At the intersection of stage and life, she weaves light and shadow through movement, transforming the joys and sorrows of life into poetry flowing through air.

Her works blend Eastern aesthetics with contemporary dance vocabulary, leading students from across Tsinghua's departments — united by their love of dance — to find their own artistic expression between tradition and modernity.

Philosophy

以身体素描天空Sketching the Sky with the Body

那些被我们称作人生的悲欢离合，
不过是大气层在亿万年尺度上一次微小的呼吸。

自然从不说话，它只用
风的流动、雨的颗粒、光的颜色
书写自己的故事。

于是我决定——
让舞者代替云去"沉默地诉说"。

What we call the joys and sorrows of life
are merely a single tiny breath
of the atmosphere across billions of years.

Nature never speaks. It writes its own story
with the flow of wind, the grain of rain,
and the color of light.

So I decided —
to let the dancers "speak in silence"
on behalf of the clouds.

— 创 作 手 记— Choreographer's Notes

当舞者的指尖划过空气留白的瞬息，
当衣摆扬起带着光影的流金，
你会突然理解——

原来云的运动就是天空的舞蹈，
而我们每个人身体里，
都藏着一片等待被唤醒的浩瀚云海。

When a dancer's fingertips trace
a fleeting pause through the air,
when fabric rises catching streams of light —

you suddenly understand:
the movement of clouds is the sky's dance,
and within each of us hides
a vast ocean of clouds waiting to be awakened.

Dialogue

对话 · 舞蹈与生命On Dance & Life

舞蹈对你意味着什么？What does dance mean to you?

在人形山川里，沉默的血翻涌成河——你要让它是真的。彼此被一种纯粹的热爱推动着，是遥不可及，又是冥冥注定，是历经千帆后的确信。用它的眼睛和身躯，去突围、去逐光、去野望。一次次醒来，一次次相信，在静穆的干燥中、在浓郁的湿润里——去写诗、去高歌、去跳舞。

In the landscape of the human form, silent blood surges into rivers — and you must make it real. We are driven by a pure, shared love — something once unreachable, yet always destined, a conviction forged after weathering countless storms. With our eyes and bodies, we break through, chase the light, dream wildly. Awakening again and again, believing again and again, in still dryness and rich moisture alike — we write poetry, we sing, we dance.

你如何看待身体与空间的关系？How do you see the relationship between body and space?

艺术从未高悬，它始终流淌在每个人的身体里。当舞者的指尖轻触展柜玻璃，文物仿佛被唤醒，与当代的呼吸产生共鸣，在静默中诉说永恒的故事。在德彪西《月光》的旋律中，提灯的舞者缓步前行，微光穿透博物馆的幽暗，如星火点亮长夜。河流是时间，而灯是永恒。这里没有观者与演者的区分，只有共同经历艺术洗礼的生命个体。博物馆的穹顶不再遥远，艺术的光芒照进了每一个普通人的日常。

Art was never meant to hang above us — it has always flowed within every body. When a dancer's fingertips brush the glass of a display case, artifacts seem to awaken, resonating with our contemporary breath, telling eternal stories in silence. To the melody of Debussy's Clair de Lune, a lantern-bearing dancer moves slowly forward, soft light piercing the museum's darkness like starfire illuminating the long night. The river is time; the lantern is eternity. Here, there is no distinction between viewer and performer — only lives transformed by art together.

忘我的时刻是什么样的？What does it feel like to lose yourself in dance?

忘我，是沉入意识的深海，触摸未被语言驯服的灵魂。舞者以身体为梭，在织绣纹样间穿行，灯火掠过之处，历史的经纬苏醒。在明清绘画展厅，肢体与山水对话：枝干舒展如笔锋，呼吸起伏似墨韵。边界在流动中松动，艺术不再是展柜内的静物，而是与生命同频的脉动。

To forget the self is to descend into the deep sea of consciousness, to touch the soul untamed by language. The dancer becomes a shuttle, weaving through embroidered patterns; where lamplight passes, the warp and weft of history awaken. In the Ming and Qing painting galleries, the body converses with landscapes: branches unfurl like brushstrokes, breathing rises and falls like ink. Boundaries loosen in the flow — art is no longer a still object in a vitrine, but a pulse beating in rhythm with life.

舞蹈如何让陌生人相连？How does dance connect strangers?

漫游者不再孤独。当多元的身体在空间中交织，当陌生的目光因艺术交汇，便成了共有的精神原乡。每个人都带着自己的故事而来，又在艺术的感召下，编织出新的集体记忆。即使终将散去，这份在艺术中获得的共鸣与感动，将成为继续前行的力量。舞者俯身时，山脉在脊背上隆起；扬手间，鱼鳍在指尖生长。万象在此和合，无界无疆——艺术终将超越形式，成为天地呼吸的节律。

The wanderer is no longer alone. When diverse bodies interweave in space, when unfamiliar gazes converge through art, they become a shared spiritual homeland. Everyone arrives carrying their own story, and under art's calling, weaves new collective memories. Even when we eventually part, the resonance found through art becomes the strength to carry on. When a dancer bends, mountains rise along the spine; in the lift of a hand, fins grow at the fingertips. All things find harmony here, boundless and infinite — art transcends form to become the breathing rhythm of heaven and earth.

Works

编创作品Choreographic Works

01

万象和合

Harmony of All Things

一部用身体"素描"天空的舞蹈。将人生境遇与云海万象交织，通过舞者的肢体哲学，探寻人生境界的心灵对话。是积雨云翻滚时的怒吼，也是雨后晴空的微笑。

A dance that "sketches" the sky with the body. Interweaving life's experiences with the myriad forms of clouds, this work explores a dialogue of the soul through the dancers' physical philosophy — the roar of cumulonimbus and the smile of a sky after rain.

演员：张尧睿 许逯彤  |  摄影：黄泓熙Dancers: Yaorui Zhang, Lutong Xu  |  Photo: Hongxi Huang

02

浩海逐光

Chasing Light Across the Sea

海纳百川，不问出处；此起彼伏，承载万物；百转千回，逆风远逐。当水流终汇进大海，平等的灵魂触碰彼岸。《浩海逐光》以纱和人的互动，描绘出一幅万物在海洋翻腾中不断积蓄能量，周而复始，生生不息的画卷。

The sea embraces all rivers, regardless of origin; rising and falling, it carries all things; through a thousand turns, it chases against the wind. When streams finally merge into the ocean, equal souls touch the far shore. Through the interplay of fabric and dancers, this work paints a tableau of all things churning within the ocean, ceaselessly gathering energy in an endless cycle of renewal.

03

生命的野望

Ambition of Life

心脏跳动迸发大地血脉，年轮流转锈刻生命辙痕。骤雨狂风，野火蔓延，百草恣意狂舞。春风拂过，万物拔节，翻涌出生命交响。舞蹈以道劲野草象征熠熠青年，与新时代同向同行，在中国大地深情野望，书写民族复兴篇章。她们是一群来自清华各院系舞蹈选修课的学生，因为对舞蹈无限的热爱而相聚。

Heartbeats pulse through the earth's veins; rings of time etch the tracks of life. Storms rage, wildfires spread, wild grasses dance unbridled. Spring winds pass, all things rise, surging into a symphony of life. The dance uses resilient wild grass as a symbol of radiant youth, marching in step with a new era. The dancers are students from across Tsinghua's departments, united by their boundless love of dance.

创作优秀奖Outstanding Choreography Award
04

Breakthrough

突破 · 突围Breaking Free

即便四肢被现实绑缚，心也扣上了枷锁，可是他们仍骄傲地昂着头。你听，不羁的灵魂在沸腾。你看，自由的生命在狂舞。"突破创新"是时代永恒的主题，舞蹈是由一卷平凡的彩色卷纸展开，它的一次次蜕变，和舞台丰富的空间运用，将演员从迷茫、到挣扎再到突破的主题层层深入地表现出来。

Even with limbs bound by reality and hearts shackled, they still hold their heads high with pride. Listen — untamed souls are burning. Look — free lives are dancing wildly. The dance unfolds from an ordinary roll of colored paper, and through its metamorphoses and rich use of stage space, the journey from confusion to struggle to breakthrough is revealed layer by layer.
